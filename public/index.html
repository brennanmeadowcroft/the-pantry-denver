We are a fully equipped, commercially licensed, shared kitchen space located close to Lo Hi Rino and with access to downtown Denver CO. We are here to help you follow your passion in creating and build your culinary dream.

Our Vision

We are starting with a shared kitchen space for fellow food creators with the vision to eventually become a kitchen incubator. We aim to helping new business take off as well as assist in business expansion. We will help coordinate with an area expert to help you plan menu, product development as well as entrepreneurship training. Our goal is to help you success.

Services

Our kitchen is fully equipped, commercially licensed. We will offer both shared space kitchen available for rent by the hour and private space available just for you and your crews. If you want to be part of us please sign up for an update.
