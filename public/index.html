The Pantry is a fully equipped, commercially licensed, shared kitchen space located close to LoHi, RiNo and with access to downtown Denver. Follow your passion in creating and build your culinary dream with affordable rates as well as access to business and culinary consulting.

Our Vision

The Pantry is a shared kitchen space for fellow food creators that aims to help new businesses take-off and expand. By connecting you with experts and customers to help you plan your menu, test new recipes and run your business, our goal is to help you succeed.

Services

In addition to a fully equipped kitchen, The Pantry will offer:

Shared Space

Enjoy affordable rates and a wide variety of equipment.

Private Space

Just you, your crew and your recipes.

Business Assistance

We will help make running your business easier so you can focus on the food.
